Discord Rules

Rule 1

Harassment or predatory behavior is not allowed, in any form.

Rule 2

Keep the chats clean & family friendly. Don’t post gore/inappropriate topics/etc.

Rule 3

No political debates or heavy / sensitive topics; keep the chat civil.

Rule 4

Do not start or participate in drama.

Rule 5

Speak English only in text and voice channels.

Rule 6

Do not tag either the owner or staff. If you have a problem, please make a ticket.

Rule 7

Don’t unnecessarily Tag other players.

Rule 8

Use channels for their dedicated purpose.

Rule 9

Do NOT impersonate staff or any other member of the community.

Rule 10

Any variation of inappropriate language, including racial slurs, whether it be non-English or otherwise, or any term used in a modern OR historical context to degrade, demean, or reduce any person of any race to a sub-class or less-than-equal stature is NOT allowed

Rule 11

No advertisement or messaging of non-related servers to people in the discord.

Rule 12

No self-advertising of YouTube or Twitch platforms without staff approval first. Please make a ticket. (This does include streaming in discord voice channels).

Rule 13

Don't join voice channels to scream/use a voice changer/spam a soundboard or any action that can be considered obnoxious and harmful to others.

Rule 14

Do not ask for compensation for items that were lost/destroyed in the game by conventional means.

Rule 15

Do not make more than one ticket for one specific issue.

Rule 16

Do not sell in-game items for real-life currency, in-game, discord, or otherwise.

Rule 17

Don't leave and rejoin the server or use an alt to get around mutes and punishments.

Rule 18

All rules apply to voice channels.

Rule 19

Your account/the account you play on is your responsibility. If someone else plays on your account/the account you play on, it is still your responsibility if they do something

Server Rules

Rule 1

Do not cheat (hacked clients, abusive mods, etc.)

Rule 2

Do not spam or flood chat.

Rule 3

Do not abuse claim trust. Unclaimed land is free game.

Rule 4

Do not bypass PvP protection plugins.

Rule 5

Stay family-friendly. No inappropriate chats, names, skins, or builds.

Rule 6

Do not claim or build within 8 chunks of a pre-existing claim. Targeting another player's claim line is not allowed.

Rule 7

Do not discuss real world, current politics, religion, illicit drugs, or other sensitive topics.

Rule 8

Predatory behavior will not be tolerated.

Rule 9

Do not harass other players. Including chat, tp spamming, and begging.

Rule 10

Do not share personal information in public chats. While we discourage sharing personal information in general, if you insist on doing so, please keep it in private messages.

Rule 11

Staff impersonation or disrespect is not permitted.

Rule 12

Do not falsely report other players.

Rule 13

Punishment or ban evasion is not allowed.

Rule 14

Do not speak other languages besides English in public chat. Local and private messages do not apply.

Rule 15

Do not advertise any other servers (Hypixel is an exception).

Rule 16

Do not intentionally start or encourage drama.

Rule 17

Do not sell in-game items for real-life currency via in-game, discord, or otherwise.

Rule 18

Do not use alt accounts. Only one account per player is allowed.

Rule 19

Do not create lag machines.
